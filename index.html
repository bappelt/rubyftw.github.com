We need you!

That's right friend, fellow enthusiast, ruby noobie, good-looking passerby, we're calling upon you to join us in spreading the goodness of ruby to anyone interested, but especially to you!

How do I get involved?

  • Join the group and join in the discussions
  • Help us find a good solid venue for regular meetings (training room? whiteboard? projector?)
  • Volunteer to speak on a subject
  • Announce impromptu meets at a nearby coffee shop or bar
  • Pitch a subject you would like to learn about
  • Follow announcements on twitter and help spread the word about the group
  • Fork this site on github and help us shine things up

When do you meet?

We had been holding the regular meetings every 2nd Tuesday of the month starting around 6pm and ending around 8pm, meaning the next one would be in 4 days on Tuesday, February the 12th, but please check the list for details as the schedule and location are a bit up in the air at the moment.
